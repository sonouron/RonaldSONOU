## 🧠 Featured Projects

### 1. 📈 Customer Churn Analysis – Fitness Subscription Service  
**Tools**: Python, Pandas, SQL, Power BI, Matplotlib, Seaborn  
**Goal**: Identify drivers of churn across 1441 subscribers and 900+ support tickets.  
**What I did**:
- Merged three datasets (customers, support tickets, engagement events)
- Built customer‑level features (engagement scores, support history, SLA breaches)
- Created interactive Power BI dashboard with attrition KPIs

**Key insight**: Support tickets and delayed resolutions are strong churn predictors, especially for Basic and Free plans.

### 2. 🔭 Nobel & Fields Medal Analysis (1901–2023)  
**Tools**: Python, BeautifulSoup, Pandas, Matplotlib, SciPy  
**Goal**: Debunk astrology myths with data — are certain zodiac signs overrepresented among top scientists?  
**What I did**:
- Scraped Wikipedia for Nobel laureates (Physics, Chemistry, Medicine) and Fields Medalists
- Cleaned and standardized birth dates, calculated zodiac signs
- Analyzed distribution by discipline, historical era, country, and season
- Applied Chi‑square tests to evaluate significance of observed patterns

**Key insight**: The observed differences are not statistically significant (p‑value > 0.05). Talent is shaped by education and opportunity, not zodiac signs.

🔗 [Repository](https://github.com/ronald-sonou/nobel-zodiac-analysis) | [Blog Series](link)
